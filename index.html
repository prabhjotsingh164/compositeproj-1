    myCard

    Click Here to add funds to yourmy card account ,which can be used to print in the college computer labs and make purchases around the college.

    All Printing at the college computer labs will be charged at the following costs:-

  • $0.07 for 8.5x11 Black and White
  • $0.09 for 8.5x14 Black and White
  • $0.14 for 11x17 Black and White

    • Students are able to add funds to their myCard Account online using a credit card or debit card. Once your myCard account is established, the balance is carried to the next semester automatically. To learn more please visit the myCard Website for the terms and conditions of your my card account.
